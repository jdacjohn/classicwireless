Tower Construction

Classic Wireless LLC is a full service tower construction & site maintenance company.

We will manage all aspects of site development including:  FAA & FCC studies, site civil construction, foundations, site grounding, tower erection, tower lighting installation and lighting maintenance, antenna & coax mounting and installation, tower painting, site audits and inspections.

We have on staff, two Anritsu Site Master certified trained technicians for all of your line and antenna sweeping projects.

Call us for a quote on covering all your quarterly site maintenance needs.

We are a member of the National Association of Tower Erectors (NATE) and we are committed to safety, quality, and total customer satisfaction.

Classic Wireless LLC has enjoyed much success erecting several different types of towers. From small face Rohn type products, monopoles, self-supporting & guyed towers. We have worked as both main contractor and sub-contractors for many of today's communication businesses in the energy, broadband and telecommunication fields. We have constructed tower sites in New Mexico, Oklahoma and Texas from 80' self support to 400' guyed towers.
